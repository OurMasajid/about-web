Privacy Policy

At Our Masajid your privacy is important to us. At the moment, we only store your email address on our servers when you create account, to maintain authorization only.

Any other information that we have access to, like your location, is just to improve usability of the application. We don't store it on our servers. However, some services we use may store this information for analytical purpose.

Any functionality of the application which allows you to enter information in text or any other format is intended to be available to other members of the community. Don't enter any information that is not intended this purpose. For example: don't enter your personal bank account number and routing on your masjid donation page.
